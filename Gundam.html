Gundam Anime

Bum-Bada-Bum! You've landed on the Gundam page of our little site. If you know mecha, Im sure you've heard of this behemoth of a series, which is often credited with defining the real robot genre. But as with any series that has been actively ongoing for over 40 years, it can be intimidating to know where to start. Have no fear, that's why we're here!

We can break Gundman down into two diffent groups. The first is Universal Century. This is the mainline series, starting with the very first entry in 1979. each entry in this series share a world and are (mostly) deeply entwined with each other. The view order for this is mostly by release, but we'll make it a little easier by listing each entry in order below. Within UC there are a few entries that take place in the far future. for the sake of convience we've seperated those entries from the more contemporate entries.

That leaves the second group, Alternate Universes These series are all self contained entries that take place in thier own standalone universes.
